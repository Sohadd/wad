Since Its opening, City Hospital. has been expanding Its
services and equipment to meet the growing healthcare
needs of the human community.
Objectives of City Hospital are to provide a tertiary level
patient care and serve as referral hospital also to conduct
teaching/ training of doctors and other health workers at
various level in the field of medicine and surgery.

The establishment of the City Hospital, Lahore heralds a
bright new era for the citizens of South Punjab, especially
the residents of the Multan.

Apart from providing medical facilities to the resident of
Multan and surrounding areas, the City Hospital will
function as a National Reference Centre for providing
specialized diagnostic and curative services to the
patients referred by other hospitals/institution.
